Honours Timetable Scheduler
===========================

## Authors:
- Justin Cossutti JUSCSS002
- Benjamin Meier MRXBEN001

## Requirements:
- CLIPS 6.X

## How to run:
- Open CLIPS console

$ (reset)
$ (clear)

$ (load "schedular.clp")
$ (batch "facts.clp")
$ (run)

$ (print_timetable)         		# to display the graphical timetable
$ (find_unplaced_lectures)			# to identify lectures that weren't placed
